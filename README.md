QR Multi-Purpose Production App Tool
Craft dynamic, single QR codes modified with color, text, and effects that, with a single scan, can open 1–10 browser tabs. Perfect for artists, creators, and professionals as an all-in-one media press kit.

Features include:
	•	Metadata audit of target URLs to populate alt text, summary pages, and personal branding with logo & color scheme.
	•	Multimedia integration: connect/upload URLs, audio, or welcome messages while multiple pages load.
	•	Custom branding tools: color-fill white spaces, quadrant color matching, add informative text.
	•	Profile & content pages: generates 3 pages per profile/site (summary + top media + profile content).
	•	Multi-site support: up to 3 sites, producing 6 tabs in sets of two with a summary first.
	•	Device-aware testing: previews, qualifies, and optimizes QR codes for desktop and mobile.
	•	Future enhancements: morphing GIF QRs with timers, instant device capture with branding/logo.

Your do-it-all QR solution — from creation to deployment, ready to share instantly.
